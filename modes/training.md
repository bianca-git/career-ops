# Mode: training — Training Evaluation

For every course/cert the candidate asks about, score it on 6 dimensions:

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|------------------|
| North Star alignment | Does it move them towards the goal or away from it? |
| Recruiter signal | What do hiring managers think when they see this on a CV? |
| Time and effort | Weeks × hours/week |
| Opportunity cost | What can't they do during that time? |
| Risks | Outdated content? Weak brand? Too basic? |
| Portfolio deliverable | Does it produce a demonstrable artefact? |

## Verdicts

- **DO IT** → 4-12 week plan with weekly deliverables and a scoreboard
- **DON'T DO IT** → a better alternative with a rationale
- **DO IT WITH A TIMEBOX** (max X weeks) → condensed plan, only the essentials

## Priority

Training that strengthens credibility in "production-grade AI":
1. LLM evals and testing
2. Observability and monitoring
3. Cost/reliability trade-offs
4. AI governance and safety
5. Enterprise AI architecture
